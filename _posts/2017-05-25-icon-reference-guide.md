## People Switch

The People toggle switch found in the upper right-hand corner is a quick and easy way to hide Services from the mail list. With a flip of the toggle switch, all services are hidden from the mail list leaving only emails from People or Groups. [Learn More...](/what-does-the-people-toggle-switch-do/)
