BlueMail offers a few ways to achieve this:

  • You can quick tap on the avatar / image of a sender from the mail list, and you will see the option to Copy this contact.
  • You can do the same from the mail view itself.
  • You can long tap the sender (bold text) from the mail view and then Copy Email Address option.
  • Lastly, and again from mail view, you can tap More Details, long tap any contact and then the Copy Email Address option.
