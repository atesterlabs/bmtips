<ul>
<li><b>Attach a File</b></li>
<li><b>Take a Picture</b></li>
<li><b>Add Cc/Bcc</b></li>
<li><b>Set High Priority</b></li>
<li><b>Enable Read Receipt</b></li>
</ul>
