  • Grab your Android device.
  • Download BlueMail app from Google Play store https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=me.bluemail.mail
  • Install it.
  • Open BlueMail app.
  • Choose your first email account (you have to configure at least one to start using the app).
  • There you Go!
