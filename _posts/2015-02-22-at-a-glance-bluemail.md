<p><b><span style="color: #000000;">What is BlueMail and all the awesome features it includes? - </span></b><span style="color: #000000;">BlueMail</span> is a free and beautifully designed universal email client that is capable of managing an unlimited number of email accounts from all sorts of providers. This allows for smart push notifications while enabling personalization across multiple email accounts. <em><span style="color: #000000;">BlueMail</span></em> is the perfect replacement for your stock email app.</p>
<h2><b>All of Your Emails in One Place</b></h2>
<hr />
<p>[caption id="attachment_4352" align="aligncenter" width="450"]<img class="wp-image-4352" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/Box_thumb.png" alt="Box_thumb" width="450" height="252" /> BlueMail handles every provider you have heard of and then some.[/caption]</p>
<p>● <a href="http://gmail.com">Gmail</a>, <a href="//mail.yahoo.com/">Yahoo mail</a>, <a href="//office.live.com/start/Outlook.aspx">Outlook</a>, <a href="//mail.aol.com/">AOL</a>, <a href="//www.icloud.com/mail">iCloud</a>, <a href="//outlook.office365.com">Office365</a>, Exchange, <a href="http://apps.google.com/‎">Google Apps</a>, Apple mail, Hotmail, <a href="http://www.msn.com/en-us">MSN</a>, Live, <a href="//mail.yandex.com/">Yandex</a>, Y!mail, <a href="//mail.ru/">Mail.ru</a>, <a href="//www.gmx.com">GMX</a>, <a href="//www.mail.com/">mail.com</a>, <a href="//www.hushmail.com">Hushmail</a>, <a href="//www.zoho.com/mail/">Zoho</a>, <a href="//web.de/">Web.de</a>, <a href="http://qip.ru/">QIP</a>, <a href="//mail.rambler.ru">Rambler</a> ...<br />
● Support for IMAP, Exchange &amp; POP3 mailbox<br />
● Sync multiple mailboxes from all your providers in a Unified Interface<br />
● Real push email for the widest range of mail services<br />
● Material Design and Lollipop</p>

<h2>Enhanced Features</h2>
<hr />
<p>[caption id="attachment_4353" align="aligncenter" width="448"]<img class="wp-image-4353" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/Dark_Theme_thumb.png" alt="Dark_Theme_thumb" width="448" height="251" /> With a powerful Unified Account experience packaged in an intuitive and easy to use design, BlueMail provides a top notch email service.[/caption]</p>
<p>● Smart Mobile Notifications - Quiet Hours, Custom Sounds, Vibrate, LED Light, Snooze Alerts and other preferences <em>per each of your accounts</em><br />
● Beautiful Dark Theme - with automatic switch between day &amp; night modes<br />
● Rich Text Signature - Easily configure text styles and add your logo<br />
● Android Wear - Smart email on your watch<br />
● Mobile Printing – Integrated with any printer supported by Android such as select plugins<br />
● Dynamic Smart Conversations – Makes email convenient and easy<br />
● Configurable Menus– Customize your swipe menu and email view actions with what matter to you the most</p>
<h2>Simplifying Email With Clusters</h2>
<hr />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>[caption id="attachment_4366" align="aligncenter" width="448"]<img class="wp-image-4366" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/Clusters.png" alt="Clusters" width="448" height="252" /> Clusters, like books, keep  your loose emails in order and in one place.[/caption]</p>
<p>● Smart Clusters – Automatically combine relevant emails into a single expanding slot<br />
● Notifications - Set different notification sounds for specific services<br />
● <b>Manage</b> - Control all emails from a specific sender easily<br />
● Multi-Edit - Use multi-edit to quickly move, delete, or Block clusters</p>

● Mark Emails as Done - When you finish handling an email, you can just mark it as done to get it out of the way without having to delete it or reach Zero Inbox by using smart filters.<br />
● Quick Filters - Easily filter emails by unread/starred</p>
